About Love Saylani

Saylani Welfare International Trust is a non-government organization (NGO) focusing primarily on feeding the poor and homeless. It was established in May 1999 and is headquartered at Bahdurabad, Karachi, Pakistan. Saylani Welfare is an organization that helps people who are in need, especially those who are poor. They believe in the importance of kindness and compassion towards others.

HealthCare

Saylani Welfare provides various services to support those less fortunate. They offer free meals to people who cannot afford to buy food, so that no one goes hungry. They also provide healthcare services, such as medical camps and free medicines, to help those who cannot afford proper medical treatment.

Vocational Training

In addition, Saylani Welfare runs vocational training programs to teach skills and empower individuals with opportunities for a better future. This includes courses on computer education, sewing and embroidery, and other practical skills that can help people find jobs or start their own businesses.

natural disasters

The organization also works towards disaster relief efforts during emergencies or natural disasters. They provide immediate aid such as food supplies, clean water, and shelter for those affected by such situations.

Saylani's Mission

Saylani Welfare's mission is to make a positive difference in the lives of the underprivileged by extending a helping hand when they need it most. Through their selfless efforts and dedication towards uplifting others from poverty, they inspire hope and bring smiles to the faces of many.
